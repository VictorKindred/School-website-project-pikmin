What is "Pikmin?"

Pikmin is a real-time strategy, puzzle game franchise by Nintendo. The games take place an unknown amount of years in the future on Earth. Mysteriously, humans are not seen and there are new types of plants and animals on the planet. You play as one inch tall "aliens" that resemble humans. Pikmins are colorful creatures with plants growing on top of their head. The player commands an army of up to 100 of these little creatures to accomplish the given goal. From escaping the planet, to collecting treasure, to even collecting fruit for food.
