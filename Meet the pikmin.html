Red Pikmin

Characteristics

  • Red coloring
  • Pointy nose
  • Strongest hit damage
  • Immune to fire

Red Pikmins are the very first pikmins that you encounter in every installment. They are characterized by their bright red coloring and their long, pointy nose. These little guys are the go-to since they are really good at taking down enemies

Yellow Pikmin

Yellow pikmins are are the second type that the player will encounter in their playthroughs. This bunch is probably one of the most gifted in regards to their versatility.

Characteristics

  • Yellow coloring
  • Big ears
  • Can be thrown further because lighter
  • Immune to electricity

Blue Pikmin

Blue pikmins are usually the last of the 3 original pikmins that the player will get. Their special ability is that they can swim. Unfortunately this is the only thing that they can do differently.

Characteristics

  • Blue coloring
  • Mouth gill
  • Can swim
